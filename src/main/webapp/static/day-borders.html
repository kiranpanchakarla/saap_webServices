Day Borders & Nurseries

Sports Nurseries
  • Launched in 2018-19.
  • Established Gymnastics in 4 districts i.e., Visakhapatnam, East Godavari, Guntur and Kurnool for children’s under Day Border Scheme in the age group of 4-8 years with a sanctioned strength of 160 @ 20 for boys and 20 for girls at each center.
  • Established Aquatics (Swimming) in 4 districts i.e., Vizianagaram, East Godavari, Chittoor and Kurnool for children’s under Day Border Scheme in the age group of 4-8 years with a sanctioned strength of 160 @ 20 for boys and 20 for girls at each center.
  • The trainees are provided coaching facility, sports equipment, sports kit and Nutrition Diet supporting @ Rs.50/- per head per day.
  • Budget Rs.297.28 Lakhs for the year 2018-19.
  • Total sanctioned strength 80 boys and 80 girls in each discipline i.e., 160 for Gymnastics and 160 for Aquatics (Swimming).

Sports Coaching Schemes – Day Boarder Coaching Scheme
  • Launched in 2018-19.
  • Two day boarder centres in each District are established to support the talented Athletes especially in Long Term Athletic Development (LTAD) Programme i.e.,25 centres and 206 players.
  • State Medallist and National participants were identified for providing monthly sports scholarships.
  • SAAP/DSA/Assn/PD/PLT’s etc., by providing coaching facility, monthly scholarship @ Rs.1,500/- to 2,500/- per head per month towards Nutrition support and Sports Kit.
  • Identified talented upcoming players of the State in the age group of 12 to 18 years i.e., in the Sub Juniors & Junior category to nurture them with regular sports training under the watchful eyes of reputed coaches of the State.
  • Budget Rs.192.88 Lakhs for the year 2017-18.

Centre of Excellence In Weight Lifting
  • The COE is aimed at producing world class Weight Lifters from Andhra Pradesh to participate in international competitions and to bring laurels to the country.
  • During the 75th SAAP Board meeting board has accepted the proposal.
  • The Govt. vide its memo no. 13057/5/2018 – 11M Sec. SAAP, Dtd: 03.12.2018 have directed to make suitable recommendation in light of experience of Athletics centre of excellence.
Infrastructure Required:
  • One main platform - 4 Mtrs x 4 Mtrs
  • 8 practice platforms (2.5 Mtrs x 2.5 Mtrs)
  • Gym hall
  • Other sports facilities like 400 Mtrs track and other play fields
Coaching Staff:
  • Chief Coach - 01
  • Assistant Coach - 02
Equipment required for COE, Weight Lifting:
  • Weight lifting sets - 4 each Men/Women
  • Three layers of platform required - 08 No’s
  • Power stands, Squat stands, Hyder Stands, Lifting Boxes, Massage chairs, step ups, Wooden benches, etc.,
