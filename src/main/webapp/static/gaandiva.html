Gaandiva

Project Gaandiva
  • Launched in 2018-19.
  • The Hon’ble Chief Minister inaugurated the project on 24th July, 2018.
  • The project is inHub & Spoke model with Centre of Excellence as Hub and 100 selected schools as Spokes for providing coaching to selected athletes and coaches by international level coaches and providing training programme and towards providing right talent to PROJECT GAANDIVA at grass root level, screening, of every eligible child of all 13 Districts.
  • 1,17,000 children belonging to age of 10 to 14 years were screened by APAA through motor ability tests for identification of talented children in AP. 5,328 talented children across AP were identified in this process.
  • 116 schools are identified as Spokes, the Grass Root Level coaching has started in 26 schools.
  • The services of the 91 coaches along with training equipment at schools are provided by M/s. Tenvic Sports Education Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore.
  • The selected students reported to the head masters on 22nd December, 2018.
  • Project is maintained by M/s. Tenvic Sports Education Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore.
  • Budget is Rs.2, 340.00 Lakhs for 2018-19.
  • The Centre of Excellence in Athletics is established at Acharya Nagarjuna University, Guntur in 2018-19.
  • The total sanctioned strength of boys and girls in COE are 75.
  • Existing strength Boys – 30 and Girls -06.
  • 14 Call Letters were sent to Athletes outside AP for admission to COE Gaandiva, 06 (Boys – 04 & Girls - 02) Athletes were joined and remaining are expected to join before 15.02.2019.
  • Present strength Boys – 34 and Girls – 08 Total - 42.
  • Till date COE Athletes participated in 7 Athletics championship held from July 2018 – Jan 2019 and won in total 75 medals.
    • Sprinter - 33 Medals
    • Middle Distance – 8 Medals
    • Jumpers – 16 Medals
    • Throwers – 18 Medals
Project Panchajanya - State Sports Academies
  • Launched in 2017-18with 7 Individual Sports Disciplines for both Boys and Girls each with sanctioned strength of 280.
  • Extended facilities to Team Games disciplines in 2018-19 with total disciplines 15 for boys and 14 for girls with sanctioned strength of 635 both.
  • Proposed to include 3 more disciplines totalling to 18 disciplines for boys and 17 disciplines for girls with total strength of 875 @ 25 trainees for each discipline.
  • Project is maintained by M/s. Tenvic Sports Education Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore.
  • The tendered amount for the year 2017-18 only for Individual Sports is Rs.540.00 Lakhs excluding taxes and for the year 2018-19 for both Individual Sports and Team Games is Rs.1,433.70 Lakhs.

Project Vijaya - Sports Adopted (Pvt.,) Schools
  • Launched in 2017-18.
  • 32 Schools are identified and now the scheme is implemented in 30 schools as per feasibility.
  • Number of disciplines covered - 8 Individual Sports and 8 Team Games.
  • Total numbers of trainees under the scheme are 3,450 both boys and girls.
  • Services of 74 Coaches are provided and maintained by M/s. Tenvic Sports Education Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore.
  • Budget is Rs.872.16 Lakhs for 2018-19.
  • Budget is Rs.516.00 Lakhs for 2017-18.
  • The trainees are provided coaching facility, sports equipment, sports kit, competition exposure and medical & insurance coverage.

Project Vijaya - Sports Adopted (Govt/ZP) Schools
  • Launched in 2018-19.
  • Department of School Education have identified 303 Government /ZP Schools covering 7 popular team game disciplines including athletics.
  • As per the assurance by Govt Sports Policy 2017-22 to get 5,000 children under regular sports training adoption of Govt/ZP Schools are mandatory.
  • At present 3,636 inmates are under training in the selected Govt/ZP schools under Long Term Athletic Development (LTAD) Programme.
  • The trainees are provided coaching facility through Physical Literacy Teachers (PLTs) of the school by providing sports equipment and sports kit to the adopted schools.
